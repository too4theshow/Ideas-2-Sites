## 💝 Life Events & Celebrations

### 🎉 Anniversary Gift
**Prompt:** "Build a website celebrating my 10th anniversary with my wife. We met at a coffee shop, got engaged at a national park, and have two kids. She loves sunflowers and hiking."

**What you get:** A cinematic timeline scroll — from the coffee shop meet-cute to the proposal to the wedding day. Warm golden palette with sunflower accents. Parallax photography. Sections for each chapter of your story.

## 🔑 The Pattern

Notice something? **Every single one of these is just a description of something real in someone's life.** You don't need to know HTML. You don't need to know design.

Just describe the **thing** — the AI handles the **design**.

The more specific details you include (names, dates, places, numbers, stories), the more personal and stunning the result.
